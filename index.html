About

Gillian Brase, the owner/operator of Retching Press, was introduced to screenprinting at a young age while attending a rock band camp in Portland, OR. She began printing shirts for local bands in highschool and attended Pratt University's bachelor of fine arts program with an emphasis in fine art prints. After college, she started Retching Press and has been printing garments and fabric for artists and fashion designers from around the world. Recent notable employers include Laika, Rita Salt, and Spielmans Bagel Company.
